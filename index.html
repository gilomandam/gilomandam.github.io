My name is Videla Lacrosse. I am the owner of Lucid Homes. Since I was a child, I dreamed of having a space a could call mine. When I grew older and exposed to modern living, I was inspired to create spaces of homes where busy homebody people could feel the beauty of home while living in the bustling city.
